How To Change a Diaper

  1. Keep diaper supplies close and easy to access in the places you would normally change a diaper.
  2. Locate a clean, secure and warm spot to change your baby
  3. Place the back half of a clean diaper under your baby
  4. Pull away the tabs of the dirty diaper
  5. Pull the dirty diaper away from your baby. .
  6. Fold up the dirty diaper and put it aside
  7. Wipe your baby's bottom with a wipe or a damp cloth.
  8. Pull up the front half of the clean diaper.
  9. Wash hands.
